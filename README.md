## â­ Key Insights

- The **Sales department**, especially **Sales Representatives**, shows the highest attrition rate.
- **Entry-level employees (Job Level 1)** experience the highest turnover rates.
- **Young employees aged 18â€“21** are the most likely to leave.
- Employees with an **average monthly income of 2,800 or less** have a significantly higher attrition rate.
- **Low-level job positions**, **overtime workers**, and those with **less than 2 years of experience** are more prone to leave.
- **Single employees** have a higher attrition rate compared to others.
- Poor **work-life balance**, **low environmental satisfaction**, and **low job satisfaction** significantly contribute to attrition.
